Welcome!

My name is Anahi and welcome to my crochet page! I was introduced to crochet since I was a little girl. My great-grandmother, my grandmother, and my mother all crochet. I however was uninterested in the art up until 2014 when I saw an amigurumi book at the library. I decided to give it another chance and I started to love every stitch. I started to learn new skills and it became second nature to me. Crochet has helped me inmensly during my post-partum depression. It helps me unwind and it makes me happy. Lately I have been teaching others to crochet at the public library. It's so cool to see people wanting to learn a new skill.
